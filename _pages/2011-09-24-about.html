I am a Senior Lecturer at the University of Gothenburg, with the division of Human-Computer Interaction in the department of applied IT. I started programming when I was 6. Got a masters from Chalmers University of Technology in CS. Got a PhD in Man-Machine Interaction from Stockholm University that I did while at Mobile Life Centre.

After my PhD i spent 4 years in Scotland as a research associate at the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow, working on things such as large-scale log analysis of apps use, wearable technology, mobile communication, and mobile wellbeing.

After I returned from Scotland in 2017I I worked for 2+ years in industry as a digital strategist consultant at Iteam helping clients think in digital ways, build software, and inspire people about the future of technology.
